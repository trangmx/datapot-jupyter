FTU JupyterHub Project

FTU & Datapot

As part of a pioneering initiative at FTU & Datapot, this JupyterHub equips educators and their FDC students. This JupyterHub, at first, aims at providing an environment for teaching Data Science courses at FTU & Datapot. This system enables educators to create and manage courses using Jupyter Notebook, also students can use this JupyterHub for practicing and doing assignments. This JupyterHub complements the plethora of educational texts available by providing resources for learning through hands-on programming and computing. Our ambition does not stop at education only, we expect to expand the Hub to support research as well. This JupyterHub will be the platform for researchers to access and use computing resources on HPC.

The groundwork for the Hub has been started by some Datapot members, this JupyterHub still has many things to be improved. We are actively seeking for collaboration and supports from other university's members. If you are interested, send us an email to Dr. Mai Xuan Trang.
